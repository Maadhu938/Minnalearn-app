Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This page applies to the MinnaLearn: Japanese N5 mobile application available on Google Play.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MinnaLearn app on your device
  2. Go to Profile (last tab)
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

You can also send a deletion request by email:

Send Deletion Request Email

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account email and display name (Firebase Authentication)
  • Study progress (lesson completion, quiz scores, streaks, study time)
  • Bookmarked vocabulary
  • Learned kanji progress
  • Achievements and game scores
  • All data stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • All data stored locally on your device

Data That May Be Retained

No personal data is retained after account deletion. Firebase may retain anonymized analytics data for up to 60 days as part of their standard processing.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed immediately when done through the app. Email requests are processed within 7 business days.

Important

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your learning progress, streaks, bookmarks, and achievements will be permanently lost.
