Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

MinnaLearn is a Japanese language learning app. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

MinnaLearn may collect and store the following information:

  • Your name and email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Study progress, including lesson completion, vocabulary learned, kanji studied, and game scores
  • App usage data such as streaks, bookmarks, and achievements

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience
  • Track your learning progress and streaks
  • Save and sync your data across devices using Firebase
  • Send local study reminders and notifications (with your permission)

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your study data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • When signed in, your data is synced to Google Firebase (Firestore)
  • Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy

4. Third-Party Services

MinnaLearn uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - for authentication, cloud database, and storage
  • Google Sign-In - for user authentication

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the app settings
  • Sign out at any time

6. Children's Privacy

MinnaLearn does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
