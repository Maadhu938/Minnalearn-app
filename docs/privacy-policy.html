Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the MinnaLearn: Japanese N5 mobile application on Google Play.

MinnaLearn is a Japanese language learning app for JLPT N5 learners. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and display name (Firebase Authentication, including Google Sign-In).
  • Learning data: Lesson completion, study time, streaks, quiz scores, bookmarked vocabulary, learned kanji, achievements.
  • Usage data: Screen views and feature usage (e.g., first_open, session_start) via Firebase Analytics.
  • Approximate location: City/region inferred by Firebase from IP; no GPS/Wi-Fi/Bluetooth location is collected.
  • Device info: App version and device type for troubleshooting. Learning assets are bundled in the app; no downloads from Firebase Storage.
  • Study progress is stored locally (SQLite) and synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore only when you are signed in.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Track learning progress and streaks.
  • Sync your data across devices.
  • Send local study reminders and streak alerts (with your permission).
  • Improve app features using aggregated analytics.

3. Data Sharing

  • Your data is processed by Google Firebase for authentication, analytics, and cloud sync.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • Advertising IDs are disabled; the app shows no ads and performs no profiling.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored locally on your device (SQLite).
  • When signed in, data is securely synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore.
  • Firebase uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

5. Third-Party Services

Provided by Google:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Analytics

Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct your personal data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Profile -> Delete Account).
  • Sign out at any time.

See our Account & Data Deletion page for details.

7. Children's Privacy

MinnaLearn does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us. The app is rated IARC 3+ and contains no ads.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
