**App Name:** MinnaLearn

**Short Description (under 80 chars):**

**Full Description:**
MinnaLearn Japanese N5

Learn Japanese N5 with a complete study companion:
• 25 structured vocabulary lessons
• 100 JLPT N5 kanji with readings
• Grammar explanations for each lesson
• Flashcards with text-to-speech
• Interactive mini-games (Matching, True/False, Typing, Kana Puzzle)
• Quiz mode with progress tracking
• Kanji writing practice with stroke tracing
• Daily study streak tracking
• Unlockable achievements
• Cloud sync with Firebase
• Daily study reminders
