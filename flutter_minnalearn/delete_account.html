Account & Data Deletion Request

How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the MinnaLearn app on your device
  2. Go to Profile (last tab)
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

Alternatively, you can send a deletion request by email:

Send Deletion Request Email

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account email and display name
  • Study progress (lessons, quizzes, streaks)
  • Bookmarked vocabulary
  • Learned kanji progress
  • Study statistics and achievements

Data That May Be Retained

No personal data is retained after account deletion. Firebase may retain anonymized analytics data for up to 60 days as part of their standard processing.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed immediately when done through the app. Email requests are processed within 7 business days.

Important

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your learning progress, streaks, and bookmarks will be permanently lost.
